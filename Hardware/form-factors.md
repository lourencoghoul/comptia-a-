#  Form Factors

##  What is it?

Form factors are standards that define the size, shape, and specifications of hardware components.

---

##  Common Case Sizes

* **Full Tower** (ATX, E-ATX)
* **Mid Tower** (ATX, E-ATX)
* **Mini Tower** (Micro-ATX, Mini-ITX)
* **SFF (Small Form Factor)** (Mini-ITX)

---

##  Importance

* Defines component compatibility
* Influences case size
* Affects cooling and performance

---

##  Note

Each form factor has its own physical and electrical specifications.
